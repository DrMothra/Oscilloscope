Oscilloscope Instructions

Welcome to the oscilloscope application page, here you'll find all the instructions to visualise your data.
At the moment the app will only visualise data from pubnub, hopefully in the future it will visualise data from other sources.

Enter the name of the relevant pubnub channel in the channel name at the top left and press enter.
You'll then receive a message asking you to wait until the app has retrieved all the information from pubnub about your channel.
If this is successful then the stream names will become populated with the relevant information.
If you then click the Enable button next to the relevant stream then you should see the data being displayed, with the numeric value shown in the black box.

The jogger wheels do the following:
Amplitude - scales the output accordingly
Time - scales the time axis accordingly
Y-Shift - moves the data up and down the screen.

If you can't see your data and one of the arrows is illuminated next to your enabled channel then it means that the values are beyond the currently displayed limits.
If you adjust the Amplitude jogger wheel then it should come into view, or alternatively use the Y-Shift to move the data around the screen.
